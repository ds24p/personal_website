Welcome

Hi! I’m Dorota.

I come from Poland, but these days I live in Germany.
I’m studying Computer Science at the University of Konstanz and taking part in the “Przyszły Programista” (“The Future Programmer”) project. I’m especially fascinated by algorithms and interactive systems — I love how they connect logic with creativity.

Before diving into computer science, I studied psychology, which sparked my curiosity about how the human brain works. Neuroscience still feels very close to my heart, and I dream of finding ways to bring these worlds together.
I’m passionate about creating ideas that help people use technology in more natural and meaningful ways.

When I’m not studying or coding, you’ll probably find me out in nature, bouldering, singing, or making art.
